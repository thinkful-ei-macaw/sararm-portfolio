About Me

I have a background in science, but I have an even stronger background solving puzzles. I love solving problems and fixing things.
To me programming feels like playing a game - every time I do something new I gain experience points and eventually level up. The best part is: there’s no game overs. I can always eventually find the next step to figuring out how to solve the problem, no matter how large the problem may be (i.e. boss fight!).
I look forward to being a part of a team, so we can play(oops, I mean work) multiplayer co-op mode!
